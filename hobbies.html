Amature Ramen Connoisseur

In my travels around the world and expeditions into Los Angles my home city, I have had my share of Ramen. As such, I am somewhat of a self delcared Ramen Connoisseur. With trips to Japan, China, Spain, Brazil, Boston, Oregon I will list bellow the best bowls of Ramen I've eaten

  1. Afui Ramen, Portland Oregon
    • Very light and citrus-esque broth
    • Rainy vibes of the city
  2. 珍云 - Chin un, Tokyo, Japan
    • Random "hole in the wall" resturant that made each bowl right in front of you
    • Perfect Shouyu broth and gave off great "family biz" type vibes
